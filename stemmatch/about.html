About Us

STEM Match ENGAGE. INSPIRE. INNOVATE.

"Stem Match is here to bridge the gap between education and industry. We are passionate about developing the STEM talent pipeline, and we want to match STEM workers with great jobs."

Who We Are

The STEM fields are Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathmatics. STEM workers are everywhere. They are striving to understand our world at every level, from discovering new life in our oceans to tackling global warming and famine. Thanks to these brilliant minds we have computers, modern transportation and the fuel to run our lives.

Our team brings decades of staffing and recruiting experience, including 20 years in diversity and inclusion. We know how to bridge the STEM gap because we know what industry needs. Our approach covers a STEM worker’s career path, beginning at education through the hiring process. We know there is a shortage of qualified workers across the four industries and we are working hard to match STEM workers with great jobs. Stem Match is so much more than a staffing and recruiting company. We are an online community where generations of STEM workers can build a professional network, meet mentors and—most importantly—find jobs.
