What is STEM? ENGAGE INSPIRE INNOVATE

“The STEM fields are science, technology, engineering and mathematics. STEM workers are everywhere. They are striving to understand our world at every level, from discovering new life in our oceans to tackling global warming and famine. Thanks to these brilliant minds we have computers, modern transportation and the fuel to run our lives.”

Members

“Stem Match is a community for generations of STEM talent. Whether you are still in school, recently entered the workforce or have been in the industry for decades — you belong here. Stem Match is developing a network of students and workers by matching them with great jobs, peers and professional mentors.”

Colleges

“Stem Match is committed to developing the STEM pipeline. Through partnerships with colleges and universities, we are placing students in internships and matching recent grads with employers across the science, technology, engineering and math disciplines. We welcome colleges to develop their programs through a relationship with Stem Match.”

Employers

“Stem Match offers employers access to some of the nation's most talented STEM workers. We are committed to finding the best fit for internships, apprenticeships, full- and part-time jobs. Through a relationship with Stem Match, employers get skilled, knowledgeable workers who are a good fit in their company.”
